### MS THESIS PROJECT

I ended up doing 2 projects in one year!!

#### PROJECT 1

Looking at the conseved non-coding elements, gene position effect and how they affect gene expression and other things like replication timing, histone marks. This was studied in multiple species down the evolution. [This thesis project was a subset of that publication on convsered noncoding elements I have]

#### PROJECT 2

SOCIAL DYNAMICS at the Institute. This was a network theory / graphs based project where I studied the social dynamics in multiple bipartite [student + professors] graphs. The graphs were created based on an intitute survey conducted by us where we asked them: 

- name 4 people who would you borrow money from
- name 4 peaople you would go to if you need help with studies
- name 4 people who you would like to hang out with
- name 4 peaople who you would like play sports with
- Name 4 of ours closest friends

Then we collected academic performance data, demogrphic data from the office.

We also conducted the big five personality test for all of the students.

The next thing was to create graphs based on those surveys, and then study how these change with different questions. whether different personalities cluster differently. what does the unuspervised clustered PCA data clusters or KNN clusters signify. It was a multivariate - multimodal network study and the dynamics of it.
